Sonic X Reebok

16 December 2024


Shoes, Knuckles edition

This post is dedicated to the Sonic x Reebok shoes I recieved for my birthday. They're the Knuckles edition shoes and dang, they look so good! I've never put on a pair of shoes and thought "Damn, these are cool", but I'm a changed man now.


The tongue, which I definitely knew the name of without looking up

You can barely see it here, but the insole also has the classic checkerboard pattern too. Very nice.


A bonus life

In the official images the suede, I assume it's suede anyway, looked way to fuzzy, but in person it's great. Much less prominent.


Two small surprisingly hard and sharp spikes

If it wasn't for these small spikes, you couldn't tell there was anything Knuckles about these shoes.


Weirdly low resolution box art

One odd detail was the knockoff looking box, what's with the extremely low resolution art? Not the greatest first impression.

Okay, that's it. I love the shoes. Thank you, goodbye.
