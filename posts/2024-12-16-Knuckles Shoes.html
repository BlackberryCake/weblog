Knuckles Shoes

16 December 2024


Knuckle's Shoes!

This post is dedicated to the Sonic x Reebok shoes I got for my birthday. These are the Knuckles edition shoes and dang, they look so good! I've never put on a pair of shoes and thought "Damn, these are cool", but I'm a changed man now. Please enjoy these small photos.


The tongue, which I definitely knew without looking it up

You can barely see it here, but the inside is has the classic checkerboard pattern too. Very nice.


A bonus life

In the official images the suede, I assume it's suede anyway, looked way to fuzzy, but in person it's great.


Two small surprisingly hard and sharp spikes

If it wasn't for these small spikes, you couldn't tell there was anything Knuckles about these shoes.


Weirdly low resolution box art

One odd detail was the knockoff looking box, what's with the extremely low resolution art? Not the greatest first impression.
